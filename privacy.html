Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

TL;DR: VoiceType processes all audio locally on your Mac. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your voice data or transcriptions. Ever.

What we collect

Nothing. VoiceType does not collect any personal information, usage data, or analytics. There are no accounts, no telemetry, and no network requests to our servers.

How speech recognition works

VoiceType uses WhisperKit, an on-device AI model, to convert your voice to text. This means:

  • All audio processing happens entirely on your Mac
  • Your voice recordings are never sent to any server
  • Transcriptions are never stored or logged
  • The app works completely offline after initial model download

Permissions we require

VoiceType needs two permissions to function:

  • Microphone Access: To hear your voice for transcription. Audio is processed in real-time and immediately discarded.
  • Accessibility Access: To detect the Fn key globally and insert text at your cursor position.

Network usage

The only network request VoiceType makes is to download the Whisper AI model on first launch (~150MB). After that, the app works entirely offline.

Data storage

VoiceType stores only your preferences (like model selection) locally using macOS's standard UserDefaults. No audio, transcriptions, or personal data is ever stored.

Third-party services

VoiceType does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
